Who Is Asker Jeukendrup?

Asker Jeukendrup, PhD, is one of the most influential sport nutrition researchers in endurance sport. His research laid the foundation for modern carbohydrate fueling strategies.

His work clarified how carbohydrates are absorbed, oxidized, and limited during exercise, directly influencing recommendations used today by elite and amateur athletes.
